From Hong Kong to Toronto

Born and raised in Hong Kong, I started my career in digital services at A.S. Watson Group, analyzing performance for a loyalty app with over two million users. In 2022 I moved to Canada and rebuilt from the ground up — taking frontline customer-operations work while doing business analysis for an eCommerce consultancy, then completing a postgraduate diploma in Business Analysis at George Brown (Dean's Honour List, every term).

Today I'm the eCommerce Business Analyst at LG Electronics Canada, owning site-wide performance analysis for LG.ca. The winding route taught me something dashboards can't: how real users talk, where processes actually break, and why the "obvious" metric is often the wrong one. It's also the direction I'm building toward — from analysis into product management and ownership.

Outside work, I'm usually studying German, or hunting for fossils on Lake Ontario beaches.
